Calm Companion

Calm Companion is your handy reference to anxiety relief techniques and CBT tools to help you when your struggling.

Our goal is to provide you with the tools you need to help you manage your anxiety and stress.

The app will include a variety of tools and techniques to help you manage your anxiety and stress. These will include both short term fixes and resources to find longer term help.
